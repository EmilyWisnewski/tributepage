ABOUT

The Bernese Mountain Dog is a beautiful and sweet breed built for hard work. They are generally chill but love a good spout of exercise as well. They live to please and are filled with love. Bernese Mountain Dogs can be over 27 inches tall at the shoulder, and are covered in a thick, silky, long, coat of black, brown, and white. This breed loves cold weather and was bred for multitasking on farms in Switzerland. They are gentle with children and are amazing family dogs. Berners are silly dogs, and are a breed that just want to love and please their best friends/owners.

HEALTH

Bernese Mountain dogs are generally healthy dogs, overall. Responsible breeders should screen for health conditions such as hip and elbow dysplasia, blood disorders, some cancers, and progressive retinal atrophy. They are somewhat susceptible to bloat as well. Ears should also be checked regularly for infection and their teeth should be brushed often.
